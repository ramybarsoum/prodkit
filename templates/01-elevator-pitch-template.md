# Elevator Pitch Template

> **What this is:** The shortest possible artifact that captures the "what and why" of a product or initiative. Two sentences max. Written before any other document. Used in every meeting, deck, and kickoff.
>
> **When to use:** Before writing a PRD. Before a strategy session. When onboarding anyone new to the initiative.

## Quality Check

- [ ] Pitch fits in 2 sentences
- [ ] A non-expert understands it without follow-up questions
- [ ] North KPI is measurable and specific
- [ ] Interim KPI is visible within 90 days
- [ ] Both KPIs have baselines (or "not yet tracked" with a plan to track)
